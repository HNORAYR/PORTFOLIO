AUTOBIOGRAPHY

I was born in 1982. in the village of Darbas, Sisiani region, RA. I studied at the local secondary school. After graduation, in 1999 I entered the history department of Artsakh State University, Faculty of Humanities. 2001-2003 I served in the RA Armed Forces. After the service, I continued my studies in the department of history. 2006 I received a master's degree and entered a postgraduate course. I am the author of 7 scientific articles. Since 2009, I have worked in Artsakh public television's DAY program. In parallel, I had author programs: Orakhndir, Sunday Day program, a series of round tables dedicated to the 20th anniversary of the declaration of NKR, a series of programs dedicated to the 20th anniversary of the liberation of Shushi, co-authored by Ara Vanyan. Since 2011, I have been the author-host of the Real segment program. Since 2018, I have been the senior editor of the news department of the same company, since 2019, the acting head of the news service, and in 2020-2023, the head of the news service. I coordinated the Day Debate, Day Day programs, hosted the Day Interview program. Since 2014, I have been a correspondent of Armenian Armnews TV in Artsakh. Correspondent of Armenia TV in Artsakh since 2016. 2015-2018: Correspondent in Artsakh for Zartonk daily newspaper published in Lebanon.
